About Secret Manager

What is Secret Manager?

Secret Manager is a secure and easy-to-use web application designed to help you store and manage your sensitive information, such as passwords, API keys, and other confidential data. With Secret Manager, you can keep your secrets safe, organized, and easily accessible when you need them.

How to Use Secret Manager

Using Secret Manager is simple. Follow these steps:

  1. Create an account or log in to your existing account.
  2. Add a new secret by providing a name and the associated confidential information.
  3. Retrieve your secrets when needed, and Secret Manager will ensure they remain secure.
